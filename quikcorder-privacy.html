Privacy Policy

Quikcorder by Semi Modular Software
Effective date: March 26, 2026

Overview

Quikcorder is a professional audio recording app for iOS. We built it with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains clearly what data we collect, what we don't, and how your recordings are handled.

Microphone access

Quikcorder requires access to your device's microphone to record audio. This access is used solely to capture audio at your explicit direction — when you tap the record button. The microphone is never accessed without your knowledge.

By default, the microphone is only active while the app is open and in the foreground. An optional Background Listening feature in Settings allows the microphone to remain active when the app is backgrounded, enabling the pre-roll buffer to stay warm. This feature is off by default and must be explicitly enabled by you.

iOS displays a microphone indicator in the status bar whenever the microphone is active, giving you complete visibility into when audio is being captured.

Your recordings

Your recordings are stored in two places:

  • Temporarily on your device while a recording is in progress
  • In your personal iCloud Drive after you save a recording

Recordings saved to iCloud Drive are stored in your own Apple iCloud account, under iCloud Drive / Quikcorder. Semi Modular Software has no access to your iCloud Drive or any files stored there. We do not upload, transmit, collect, or have any access to your audio recordings at any time.

Data we do not collect

Quikcorder does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Audio recordings or any audio data
  • Personal information such as name, email, or location
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to us

iCloud

Quikcorder uses Apple's iCloud Drive to sync your recordings across your devices. This sync is handled entirely by Apple's iCloud infrastructure. Quikcorder does not operate any servers and has no visibility into your iCloud data. Apple's iCloud privacy policy can be found at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Third-party services

Quikcorder does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. No third-party SDKs are included in the app.

Children's privacy

Quikcorder does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app does not contain advertising or in-app purchases.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
